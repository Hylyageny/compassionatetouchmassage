Pregnancy Massage

Safe and highly beneficial massage tailored for women after their first trimester.

  • Reduced back & joint pain, better sleep
  • Improved circulation & reduced edema

Wellness through Improved Lymphatic Massage (MLD)

Our health depends on a strong immune system, and is directly affected by our lymphatic system. This is a light, relaxing therapy that improves lymphatic flow. Highly recommended for pre and post cosmetic surgeries (50-55mins).

PLEASE NOTE: IF YOU ARE COMING FOR POST MLD I WILL NEED DOCTOR'S POST INSTRUCTIONS.

Benefits of MLD to cosmetic surgery patients:

  • Bruising reduction
  • Edema reduction
  • Pain management
  • Scar-tissue prevention and fibrosis reduction
  • Infection prevention
